# CannaScope Beta V5 — Release Notes

**Tag:** `v5.0.0-beta`
**Title:** CannaScope Beta V5

CannaScope Beta V5 is the first standardized public release of the CannaScope Connecticut cannabis transparency report.

## Highlights

- **Standardized the public version name** to *CannaScope Beta V5* (internal development used "V7" naming; preserved in history).
- **Added Testing Date** throughout every product table (COA test/sample date, never the report-generation date).
- **Added CT Legal Limit and CannaScope Limit comparisons** — every row shows Measured, CT Limit, CT % Of Limit, CannaScope Limit, and Difference From CannaScope.
- **Added Producer and Lab trend summaries.**
- **Added a High Cannabinoid Content / High THC Content review section** (non-infused flower above 35%).
- **Added an Infused & Extract Potency Comparison Reference** section.
- **Added possible-remediation wording safeguards** — every remediation reference is phrased "Possible Remediation" and explicitly states it is *not* proof of remediation.
- **Improved clickable COA references** on every finding.
- **Improved validation and diagnostics** (PASS / PASS WITH WARNINGS / DRAFT / FAIL; zero-result verification; source-verified producer/DBA identity).
- **Preserved older beta builds** — no version was deleted.

## Running

Unzip the package for your OS and run the launcher (`run.bat` on Windows, `./run.sh` on macOS/Linux). Python 3.10+ required. See [`README.md`](README.md).

## Disclaimer

Every flag is a lead, not a conclusion. CannaScope Beta V5 does not claim fraud, unsafe product, or legal failure unless the live COA and the applicable Connecticut legal limit directly support that claim.
